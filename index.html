About Me

  Hi, my name is Michalis Mouzakitis and I'm a Front-End Web Developer and Designer. I was born in Chalkida, Evia, on the 18 of August 1989. Now I live in Athens, Greece.
 When I was 7 I started playing video games. That was my first approach to computers and gaming-consoles. I was a passionate gamer and really curious about how the graphics were made. This lead me at the age of 17, upon finishing high-school, to join a 3D animation college where I was educated on the basic tools and options of 3D Studio Max and Photoshop. Following my design studies, I graduated from an Electrical Engineering technical school and have experience working as an electrical apprentice, taking on the family business.
 However my passion for design lead me to follow-up on my previous studies and I became interested in web design, finding it fascinating from the beginning! It is something I have loved from the beginning, especially the fact that I am learning something new everyday. Additionally I have enrolled to a programme offered by the University of Athens titled "Web Developer" that helped me to significantly enhance my knowledge of Javascript, jQuery, Python, Bootstrap and AJAX.
 I have been working since 11/16 as a full-time Front-End Web Developer for Contadd and you can see a detailed portfolio both of my work at Contadd and in the area of 2D/3D Design further down this page.
 Thank you for your interest in me.
